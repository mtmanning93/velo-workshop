Our Team

Here at Velo Workshop we are passionate enthusiasts when it comes to bikes. Both in and out of the workshop. Our team is full of knowledge with some of the highest attainable qualifications. Outside of the workshop our team have years of riding experience between them. We know how important riding is, thats why our promise is to get you riding again as soon as possible!
